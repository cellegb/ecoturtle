Our Mission

We want to raise awareness for certain environmental issues we are facing today. Our area of focus is air, water and plastic pollution. Explore our website to learn more about environmental problems, play fun learning games, and find easy ways that you can help such as volunteering opportunities or methods you can implement into your daily life!
